Our Workflow

The Construction Journey

From the first blueprint to the final handover, we follow a rigorous 10-step process to ensure quality and precision.

01

Initial Consultation

Understanding client needs, project scope, and feasibility studies.

02

Site Survey & Analysis

Comprehensive topographical and soil analysis of the construction site.

03

Architectural Design

Drafting conceptual designs and detailed architectural blueprints.

04

Structural Engineering

Calculating load-bearing requirements and structural integrity plans.

05

Approval & Permitting

Navigating regulatory requirements and securing necessary building permits.

06

Foundation & Earthwork

Excavation and laying of solid foundations for the structure.

07

Structural Framing

Erection of the building's skeleton, including beams and columns.

08

MEP Installations

Mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems installation.

09

Finishing & Aesthetics

Interior and exterior finishing, including tiling, painting, and fixtures.

10

Final Handover

Quality assurance checks and official handover of the completed project.
